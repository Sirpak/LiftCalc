LiftCalc

LiftCalc is an online WebFit application. Specifically, a calculator that will tell you the ideal weight and rep range you want to use based on your fitness goals.

Enter the Heaviest amount you can lift one time. If you don't know, try to approximate. i.e. if your Bench Press max is 315lb, enter 315. If you can curl a 60lb dumbbell one time, enter 60. The calculator will till you how much weight to pick up, how many reps you should aim for, and how much rest to take on that exercise.
